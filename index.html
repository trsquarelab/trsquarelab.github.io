All Men's Morris

This game has three variants more to the classic nine men's morris game, namely three men's morris, six men's morris and twelve men's morris.

Three men's morris is a variant of "Tic Tac Toe" where you can move coins once all the three are placed.

The Zero Game

Aim of this game is to form as may square as possible, you win if you more than that of your opponent.

You can join zeros vertically up or down and also horizontally right or left.

A cutdown version is available to play on web browser.

Rummy Scorer

This is not a game, but a scorer application.

This will help you record your rummy games scores.
